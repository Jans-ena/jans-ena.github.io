Getting Here

Flights

You can fly into either airport — choose whichever is cheaper or more convenient:

  • San Jose (SJC) — Closer to venue, ~$21 Uber to downtown
  • San Francisco (SFO) — ~$80 Uber, or take BART → Caltrain (~$15, 1 hour)

We recommend arriving Friday, March 13th. If you're in the rehearsal, please arrive by the 12th.

Where to Stay

We recommend staying in San Jose or nearby suburbs. You could also stay in San Francisco if you want to sightsee!

Budget-friendly option: La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham San Jose Airport-Downtown — 7 min drive to the church, ~$300 for 2 nights.
